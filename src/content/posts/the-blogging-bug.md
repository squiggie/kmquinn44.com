<p>It's finally happened. I've caught the blogging bug and decided to launch my own blog. But before I embark on this journey, I want to explain my reasons for starting it.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You might wonder why I would choose to create a blog while I'm in the midst of writing the third book in the Torn Robes series, titled "The King’s Highway." </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Why indeed?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Full disclosure…as an author, I love immersing myself in the fictional world I’ve created, but aside from my family, no one gets to see this side of me—my passion and excitement as I become captivated by the characters and the directions they take me. Why did I choose the Northeast coast as the setting for the many characters who bring the Torn Robes saga to life? And while we’re at it, why did I choose the title Torn Robes?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>These questions and many more will be answered on my blog. Each post will provide new insights into the characters: what makes them who they are and why the special town of Gilford matters.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Stay tuned for my upcoming post titled "Why, Torn Robes?" as we dive into the book series <strong>"Torn Robes: A Journey Through the Characters and the Town."</strong> </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I can't wait!</p>
