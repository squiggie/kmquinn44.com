<p>Jesus said to Pilate, "You are right in saying I am a king. In fact, for this reason, I was born and for this I came into the world, to testify to the truth. Everyone on the side of truth listens to me."</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>"What is truth?" Pilate asked.  (John 18:37:38)</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In today's age, we live in a world where information is readily available at the click of a button. The velocity at which news spreads seems to be escalating with each passing day. It is crucial that we exercise caution in distinguishing between fact and fiction. Is it possible to maintain an open mind, question our own judgments, and engage in constructive conversations? </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>These are my truths: The Lord of the Heavens created me. I am of royal birth as a child of the Most High King. Jesus, God's son, is the way, the truth, and the life.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>With the guidance and strength of Jesus' love, I can achieve anything that comes my way. His unwavering support and infinite wisdom serve as my constant source of inspiration, giving me the confidence to tackle any challenge that lies ahead. With Jesus by my side, I know I can overcome obstacles and achieve great things. His love guides me on my journey and fills my heart with joy and peace.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>What is your Truth?</p>
