<p>Do we use ‘cookies'?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p>Yes. For example, we use cookies to help us compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We use cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions. We may also use trusted third-party services to track this information on our behalf. You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent or can choose to turn off all cookies. You can do this through your browser settings. Each browser is set up a little differently so please read your browser's Help Menu to learn the correct way to modify your cookies.</p>
