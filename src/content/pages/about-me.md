<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Meet K.M. Quinn</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"typography":{"fontSize":"1.2rem"},"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color" style="font-size:1.2rem">K.M. Quinn lives on the east coast where she was born and raised. She is a devoted wife, mother, and grandmother. K.M. began writing her first novel Torn Robes: Choices in 2004 between work and raising a family. The second novel in the series Torn Robes: Reckoning will be released in the Fall of 2023.<br>Along with writing her next project, K.M. enjoys spending time with her family, horseback riding, kayaking, walking, snowshoeing, and being at the beach. Having grown up along the coast the ocean has been her constant companion and still to this day her, “happy place.” She is an avid researcher and loves history along with learning and exploring new things.<br>K.M. firmly believes that with God everything is possible she has witnessed it firsthand in her own life. Moving from one career to the next God has been preparing K.M. for such a time as this. She finds it an absolute joy, pleasure, and honor to share God’s undying love with her readers.</p>
